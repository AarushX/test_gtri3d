FTC Ri3D

FTC Ri3D is our initiative dedicated to the FIRST Tech Challenge, where high school students and emerging engineers embark on their first competitive robotics journey. Through scaled innovations, hands-on mentorship, and a competitive spirit, FTC Ri3D lays the foundation for the next generation of robotic talent.

FRC Ri3D

FRC Ri3D represents our flagship collegiate program within the FIRST Robotics Competition. Here, multidisciplinary teams collaborate under high-pressure conditions to design, build, and refine a full-scale competition robot in just 72 hours. Our approach emphasizes advanced engineering, strategic gameplay, and leadership development, preparing participants for success on the competition field and in their future engineering careers.
