Welcome to Georgia Tech's Robot in 3 Days

In our inaugural year, Georgia Tech's Ri3D team came together for the first time to take on the FIRST Robotics Competition challenge. We built a complete robot within 72 hours, documented every step, and shared our journey to help and inspire the FRC community.

About GT Robot in 3 Days

Georgia Tech's Ri3D team brings together students, alumni, and robotics enthusiasts to tackle the FIRST Robotics Competition challenge head-on, right after the annual game reveal.

  • Rapid prototyping and full robot build in just three days
  • Open sharing of CAD, code, and build strategies
  • Focus on accessibility and inspiration for all FRC teams
  • Live streams, Q&A, and educational content throughout the build
