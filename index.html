About the Team

We are Georgia Tech’s premier team dedicated to the FRC FIRST robotics challenge. In just three days, we conceptualize, design, and build a robot that pushes the boundaries of modern engineering. With passion for innovation and a drive for excellence, our mission is to inspire and empower the next generation of STEM leaders.

Our Team

Our collective of visionary engineers, creative designers, and tenacious leaders come together with a unified drive. Each member contributes unique skills to solve complex challenges, innovate relentlessly, and craft a robot that stands as a beacon of collaborative spirit and modern technology.

The Robot

The robot is a masterpiece of rapid design and engineering. Merging cutting‐edge technology with functional design, each component is crafted to perfection. This machine showcases our team’s expertise, adaptability, and determination under pressure – a true symbol of our competitive spirit.
